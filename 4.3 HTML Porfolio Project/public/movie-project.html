TOP SHOWS ACCORDING TO RICH

My Top 5 Movies

Terminator

Year of Release: 1984
Production: Directed by James Cameron
About: A cyborg assassin known as the Terminator is sent back in time to kill Sarah Connor, whose son will one day lead a resistance against machines that rule the future.
Main Character: The Terminator (played by Arnold Schwarzenegger).

Transformers

Year of Release: 2007
Production: Directed by Michael Bay
About: Alien robots, the Autobots, wage war against their enemies, the Decepticons, with Earth as their battleground. A teenager unknowingly holds the key to their power.
Main Character: Sam Witwicky (played by Shia LaBeouf).

Dungeons and Dragons: Honor Among Thieves

Year of Release: 2023
Production: Directed by Jonathan Goldstein and John Francis Daley
About: A group of unlikely adventurers embarks on a quest to recover a lost relic, facing epic battles and magical creatures along the way.
Main Character: Edgin the Bard (played by Chris Pine).

Avengers

Year of Release: 2012
Production: Directed by Joss Whedon
About: Earth's mightiest heroes, including Iron Man, Captain America, and Thor, unite to stop the villain Loki and his army from enslaving humanity.
Main Character: Iron Man (played by Robert Downey Jr.).

Batman

Year of Release: 1989
Production: Directed by Tim Burton
About: The Caped Crusader fights to protect Gotham City from crime while facing off against his archenemy, the Joker.
Main Character: Bruce Wayne / Batman (played by Michael Keaton).

Top 5 Series

Gen V

Year of Release: 2023
Production: Created by Craig Rosenberg, Evan Goldberg, and Eric Kripke
About: Set in the universe of "The Boys," this series follows young superheroes attending a prestigious university as they compete to prove their worth.
Main Character: Marie Moreau (played by Jaz Sinclair).

The Boys

Year of Release: 2019
Production: Created by Eric Kripke
About: A group of vigilantes takes on corrupt superheroes who abuse their powers for personal gain.
Main Character: Billy Butcher (played by Karl Urban).

Umbrella Academy

Year of Release: 2019
Production: Created by Steve Blackman
About: A dysfunctional family of adopted superhero siblings reunites to prevent the apocalypse, while dealing with personal and family issues.
Main Character: Number Five (played by Aidan Gallagher).

Young Justice

Year of Release: 2010
Production: Created by Greg Weisman and Brandon Vietti
About: The series follows a team of young superheroes as they face villains and navigate growing up, balancing life as sidekicks and leaders in their own right.
Main Character: Nightwing / Dick Grayson (voiced by Jesse McCartney).

Warrior Nun

Year of Release: 2020
Production: Created by Simon Barry
About: A young woman wakes up in a morgue with a divine artifact embedded in her back, and she is thrust into a war between good and evil forces.
Main Character: Ava Silva (played by Alba Baptista).

Top 5 Animes

Berserk

Year of Release: 1997 (original series)
Production: Directed by Naohito Takahashi
About: The dark fantasy follows Guts, a mercenary with a troubled past, as he battles demonic forces and seeks revenge on his former best friend.
Main Character: Guts.

Monster

Year of Release:2004
Production: Directed by Masayuki Kojima
About: A psychological thriller about a doctor whose decision to save a child's life spirals into a nightmare, as the child grows into a monstrous serial killer.
Main Character:Dr. Kenzo Tenma.

Erased

Year of Release: 2016
Production: Directed by Tomohiko Itō
About:A man with the ability to travel back in time uses his powers to prevent tragedies, including the murder of his childhood classmates.
Main Character: Satoru Fujinuma.

Chainsaw Man

Year of Release:2022
Production:Directed by Ryū Nakayama
About: A young man named Denji merges with his pet devil to become Chainsaw Man and battles devils in a dangerous world for the chance to live a better life.
Main Character: Denji.

Hellsing

Year of Release: 2001
Production: Directed by Umanosuke Iida
About: The story follows the Hellsing Organization and its powerful vampire, Alucard, as they protect England from supernatural threats.
Main Character: Alucard.
